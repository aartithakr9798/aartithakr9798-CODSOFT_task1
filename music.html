What is IndieWaves'21?

IndieWaves 2023 is India's first Hybrid festival, organised by rhytHm.It aims to bring together artists, producers and music lovers for an epic weekend filled with good vibes, happiness and fun. To be Buddh InternationalCircuiy, Muzaffarpur on 28-29 December 2023, the event will be a one-of-a-kind experience with both live audience and virtual streaming.

LineUp

PARVAAZ

Parvaaz's sound can best be described as a blend of prog/psychedelic rock with elements of folk and world music. They were on the cover of Rolling Stone India's May 2018 issuenwith the magazinencalling them "India's Most Exciting Band".

SHIRLEY SETIA

Another YouTuber star on our list,Shirley featured in the Forbes Magazine, where she was referred to as "Bollywood's Next Big Shining Sensation."Shenwearsnmany hats but the one of a musician,really stand out.

ANUV ZAIN

Anuv started his YouTube channel with a single purpose: to spread pure music.9 years later,he's creating music that is relatable on ana emotional level.His singles "Alag Asmaan" and "Baarishein" have taken the Indie music world by storm.

LUCKY ALI

As a mainstay of Indipop,Lucky Ali's colorful career has stretched over decades,with heartfelt romanticism his signature.In 2020,he won new fans when a powerful clip of him saying "O Sanam" unplugged went viral

KODALINE

Kodaline are an Irish band whose indie rock manages to feel both profoundly tranqui and soul-crushingly magestic.Their hits like "All I want" and "Love like this" have earned them comparisions to their influences like Goldplay and U2

PETER CAT

With their gypsy-like guitar rock,Peter Cat Recording Co. have been selling out indie venues since 2011.The five-piece have always been a band of musical outiaws,creating their own following.

TABA CHAKE

Hailed by critics and audiences alike,Taba Chake's music is the kind that will make you stop and listen,in a world that is forcing you to rush on.With his latest record "Bombay Dreams",he has taken the country by storm through his authentice ans nostalgic music.

VIDYA VOX

Vidya Vox brings together influences of her Indian-American heritage to create music that is refreshingly unique and comtemporary.Since launching her YouTube channel in 2015,she has amassed over 600 million views and over 6 million subscribers.
